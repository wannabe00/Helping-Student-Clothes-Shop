Meet Our CEO

Mariam Ghvinjilia

Mariam Ghvinjilia is a young, passionate and creative person with a strong love for fashion and design. She started exploring the world of luxury women’s footwear in 2022 and has been designing ever since.

At just 15, she’s already combining her interest in fashion with new skills in front-end development. As a beginner in web design, she’s taking her first steps into tech — learning to bring her creative ideas to life through code, design, and digital experiences.
